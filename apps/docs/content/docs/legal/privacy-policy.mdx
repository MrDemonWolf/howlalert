# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 12, 2026**

## Overview

HowlAlert is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect the minimum data necessary to deliver push notifications and nothing more.

## Data We Collect

**APNs Device Token** — A pseudonymous identifier provided by Apple that allows us to send push notifications to your device. This token:
- Is not linked to your identity
- Is not shared with third parties
- Is stored only in iCloud (your private CloudKit database) and transiently in our push relay

We collect no other personal data. We do not collect your name, email, location, usage patterns, or any content from your Claude sessions.

## How We Use Your Data

Your APNs device token is used solely to deliver push notifications you have requested by installing and configuring HowlAlert.

**Lawful basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)):** Processing is necessary to perform the service you've contracted for (receiving push notifications).

## Data Processors

| Processor | Purpose | Location |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Apple CloudKit | Device token sync between your devices | EU/US |
| Apple APNs | Push notification delivery | EU/US |
| Cloudflare Workers | Stateless push relay | EU/US |

## Data Retention

We retain device tokens only as long as your app is installed. Uninstalling HowlAlert removes all data.

**GDPR erasure:** Uninstall the app. No server-side deletion is needed because we store nothing server-side beyond transient push relay operations.

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, and port your data. Contact: privacy@mrdemonwolf.com
