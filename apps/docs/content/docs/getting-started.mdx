# Getting Started

## Requirements

- macOS 15 (Sequoia) or later on Apple Silicon (M-series chips)
- iOS 17 or later
- watchOS 10 or later (optional)
- Active Claude Code subscription from Anthropic

## Install the macOS App

1. Download the DMG from the [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/mrdemonwolf/howlalert/releases) page
2. Drag HowlAlert to your Applications folder
3. Launch HowlAlert — it appears as a wolf icon in your menu bar
4. Grant notification permissions when prompted

Alternatively, install via Homebrew:

```bash
brew install --cask mrdemonwolf/den/howlalert
```

## Install the iOS App

1. Download HowlAlert from the App Store
2. Open the app and allow push notifications
3. Start your 7-day free trial or subscribe to Pro

## Pairing

HowlAlert pairs automatically via your iCloud account. No login, no account creation, no QR codes.

Once both apps are installed on devices signed into the same Apple ID:
- Your Mac will appear in the iOS app within seconds
- The iOS app will show "Connected" with your Mac's name

## Apple Watch

If you have an Apple Watch paired with your iPhone:
- Install the HowlAlert watchOS app from the Watch app on your iPhone
- Add the HowlAlert complication to your watch face
- The watch receives data from your iPhone via WatchConnectivity

## What Happens Next

Once paired, HowlAlert automatically:
- Watches your Claude Code session files (`~/.claude/projects/`)
- Calculates your token usage and pace
- Sends push notifications at 60%, 85%, and 100% thresholds
- Taps your wrist when Claude finishes replying
