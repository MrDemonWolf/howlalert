## What is HowlAlert?

HowlAlert monitors your Claude Code token usage and sends push notifications to your iPhone and Apple Watch before you hit your limits.

## Requirements

- macOS 13+ (menu bar app)
- iOS 16+ (companion app, optional)
- watchOS 9+ (glance, optional)
- A Claude Code subscription (Free, Pro, Max 5x, or Max 20x)

## Installation

1. Download the latest release from the [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/mrdemonwolf/howlalert/releases)
2. Open `HowlAlert.dmg` and drag HowlAlert to your Applications folder
3. Launch HowlAlert — it appears in your menu bar as a wolf icon 🐺
4. (Optional) Install the iOS companion app and pair via CloudKit

## How it works

HowlAlert reads Claude Code's JSONL session files at `~/.claude/projects/` in real time using FSEvents. It calculates your burn rate and sends a push notification to your paired iOS device when you hit 60%, 85%, or 100% of your plan's token limit.

Your plan is detected automatically from `~/.claude/.credentials.json`.

## Privacy

HowlAlert does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data beyond relaying your APNs device token to deliver notifications. See the [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy) for details.
