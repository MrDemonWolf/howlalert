> **TL;DR** — Shows current daily cost on your watch face via complications. Mirrors iOS notifications.

## Complications

HowlAlert supports the following complication families:
- **Modular Small** — Shows daily cost as text
- **Circular Small** — Compact cost display
- **Graphic Corner** — Full-color cost gauge
- **Graphic Circular** — Circular cost view

## Notifications

When your iPhone receives a HowlAlert push notification, your Apple Watch mirrors it with a custom notification view showing the alert details.
