# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

## Overview

HowlAlert ("the App") is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your Claude Code usage data stays on your devices.

## Data Collection

### What We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect, store, or transmit your Claude Code session data to our servers
- We do **not** track your location
- We do **not** sell or share any personal information
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking SDKs

### What We Process

- **Usage metrics** (token counts, session duration) are read locally on your Mac from Claude Code's session files
- **Push notifications** are sent through Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) via our Cloudflare Worker relay. The relay is stateless — it forwards notifications and does not store your data
- **Device pairing** uses Apple's CloudKit (private database) — only accessible by devices signed in to your iCloud account

### Local Storage

- macOS: Usage snapshots cached in UserDefaults
- iOS: Usage history stored in CloudKit (private iCloud database)
- watchOS: Latest snapshot received from iOS companion

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple Push Notification Service (APNs)** — for delivering push notifications
- **Apple CloudKit** — for device pairing (private database, encrypted, Apple-managed)
- **Cloudflare Workers** — stateless relay for push notification delivery (no data retention)

## Data Retention

We do not retain any user data. The Cloudflare Worker relay processes push notifications in real-time and does not store them beyond a short-lived delivery log (last ~100 events, automatically rotated).

## Children's Privacy

HowlAlert is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and on our documentation site.
